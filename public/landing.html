FAQ

Questions, answered.

Does Sortify store my actual files?

▾

Sortify stores encrypted copies of your files on secure Indian cloud infrastructure. All files encrypted with AES-256 and we operate a Zero Knowledge policy — our team cannot access your file contents.

What file types does Sortify support?

▾

PDFs, Word documents (.docx, .doc), Excel sheets, JPGs and PNGs (with OCR), scanned documents, and plain text files. More formats added every month.

How does the AI read my documents?

▾

When you upload a file, our AI engine runs OCR (for scans/images), then applies NLP to extract entities like names, dates, amounts, and document type. This is all processed on secure servers — your data never trains public models.

Is Sortify compliant with India's DPDP Act?

▾

Yes. Sortify is built from the ground up to comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. Data is stored in India, consent records are maintained, and you can request full data deletion at any time.

Can I use Sortify offline?

▾

The desktop agent (coming Q3 2026) will support offline access to previously synced files. The web app requires an internet connection for search and AI features.

How do reminders work exactly?

▾

The AI reads expiry or renewal dates directly from inside your documents and automatically schedules notifications at 90, 30, and 7 days before the deadline via email, push notification, and in-app alert.

What happens if I exceed the free plan limit?

▾

You'll be notified before hitting the limit. Existing files remain accessible and searchable. New uploads are paused until you upgrade or remove older files — nothing is deleted automatically.

Is there a mobile app?

▾

The Android app launches with V1.0 in Q2 2026. iOS follows in Q3 2026. Both apps support camera scanning, file upload, search, and push reminders.
